Space Adventure
Choose Your Own Space Adventure!
                       
Space Adventure: A mission to inspire the next generation of explorers. Choose Your Own Space Adventure is a trivia style interactive modeled after the Apollo Missions. In it, students will be able to go through simulations ranging from astronaut training to space engineering - all the while learning more about NASA space missions. After all, space is the final frontier. What better way to experience it...than through an adventure. Enjoy!
